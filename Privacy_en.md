Privacy Policy

iNC-Mill Privacy Policy
Effective date: Nov 23, 2025

We respect your privacy. This Policy explains what data may be processed when you use iNC-Mill.

1. Data We Collect

iNC-Mill is primarily an offline, on-device app. We do not proactively collect personally identifiable information.
Limited data may include:

Subscription Entitlements
Purchases are processed by Apple. We only receive subscription entitlement status via StoreKit (active/inactive, renewal date, product ID). We do not access your Apple ID credentials or payment details.

User Files and Local Settings
NC/G-code files, preview caches, and settings are stored locally on your device or in locations you choose. We do not upload these files to our servers.

Diagnostics You Share
If you email support with logs or screenshots, you voluntarily provide them. We use them only for troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Data

We use data only to:

·Maintain subscription access;

·Improve stability and performance;

·Respond to support requests you initiate.

3. Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We do not share user files or identifiable data with third parties except Apple’s subscription/payment services. If we add third-party services in the future, we will update this Policy and notify you.

4. Security

We use reasonable safeguards, but no system is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You may delete local files, clear app data, or uninstall at any time. You may manage/cancel subscriptions in iOS Settings. You may request deletion of support materials you sent.

6. Children

The App is for general users and not intended to collect children’s data.

7. Updates

We may revise this Policy. Material changes will be communicated in-app or via release notes.
